A Page About Me

I was born and raised in the Kansas City, Missouri area.I am a mother of three and I hope to eventually own an art business!

My vision is to create a brand that incorporates explosively unique and creative artwork into clothing, houseware and more.

Because of this it will be called an "Art Company" as it will hold a vast array of art pieces. Even more importantly, I will be pushing the initative of breaking the stigma surrounding mental health by not only putting my art out to the world (as I personally suffer from mental illness) but also by hopefully persuading other artists who have mental health diagnoses to join the cause as well, as we change the narrative one art piece at a time.

To help me achieve my long-term goal, I am definitely looking to switch over into the field of information technology. The field pays well and I've always had a knack for computers. I currently do a lot of manual labor and I would absolutely love to put my brain to use, doing something that I believe is more fitting for me.
